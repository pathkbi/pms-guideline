How to process to clean

Step 1: [Housekeeper] Navigate to "Room to clean" Screen

Step 2: [Housekeeper] process to clean and mark done

Step 3: [Supervisor] double check in "Room condition" screen
Step 3.1: [Supervisor] mark "Checked" if the room is well cleaned

Step 3.2: [Supervisor] mark "Reopened" and fill the note to this room if the room is badly cleaned

Functions:

Process to start clean choosen room

Upload to upload photos of that room. For example: breaking lamp photo
