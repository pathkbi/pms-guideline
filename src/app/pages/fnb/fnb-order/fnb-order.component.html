How to order dishes

Step 1: Navigate to "Hall plan" Screen

Step 2: Open the table

Choose the table and input the number of guests

Step 3: Pick the dishes

On the left hand area, choose the dish and input the quality, thereafter click icon "+"

Step 4: Add more Modifiers (if needed)

Firstly, select the item

Secondly, navigation Modifiers tab

Finally, add the options to that dish

Functions:

Remove to delete item out of the list

Confirm to lock the dish and send notify to kitchen

Delivered already serve that dish to the customer

Cancel to void the dishes. The use have to choose a reason to void that dish
