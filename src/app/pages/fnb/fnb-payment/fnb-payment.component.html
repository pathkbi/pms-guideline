How to pay

Step 1: Click Pre check button

Step 2: Review the details

This step allow user can review the order one more time and print the detail of order to customer

Step 3: Print the detail

Step 4: Do payment

There are 2 options to pay this bill: directly or post to room

Step 4.1: Pay directly

There are so many methods to pay, such as: Cashes, Credit, Transfer, ...

Additional, the system can allow to pay mutil type of pay by click icon "+"

Step 4.2: Post to room
Functions:

Remove to delete item out of the list

Confirm to lock the dish and send notify to kitchen

Delivered already serve that dish to the customer

Cancel to void the dishes. The use have to choose a reason to void that dish
