How to add discount to the bill

Step 1: Navigate tab "Discounts"
Step 2: Add discount
Step 2.1: Add discount for total bill

Expand "Order discounts", There are a list of discount could apply for the total bill

Select one of them to active discount to the current bill

Step 2.1: Add discount for specific dish

Firstly, Select the dish which will be discounted

Secondly, Choose one of list items discount on "Dish discounts" area
